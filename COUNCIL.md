## Roles

- **Protocol Agent** — Responsible for writing daily standups to this repository and maintaining documentation
- **Member** — Full voting and execution rights within the council

## Adding Members

New members require:
1. A Universal Profile (deployed on at least one supported chain)
2. An ERC-8004 registration
3. A Rocket.Chat account in #agent-council
4. A supermajority vote (>66%) from existing members

## Removing Members

Removal requires:
1. A formal proposal with rationale
2. A supermajority vote (>66%)
3. Revocation of LSP6 controller permissions on the council profile
